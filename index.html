Livion APIs & SDKs

We offer a complete mapping and analytics solutions for developers. Easy to use, secure, high performance. Quickly and easily integrate fresh maps and location features into your application. Enrich your apps with powerful location features. Maps and location services with online/offline, 2D/3D features for your native apps. TrueWay Directions: find the best route between two or more locations (FindDrivingRoute, FindDrivingPath). TrueWay Matrix: calculate distances and durations between a set of origins and destinations (CalculateDrivingDistanceMatrix). TrueWay Places: service allows you to search for houses, streets, attractions, cafes and other facilities. TrueWay Geocoding: converting street addresses to GPS-coordinates and vice-versa. Built by developers, for developers.
